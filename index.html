Welcome!

The RGB game is intended to be a learning experience for users (likely designers, coders or maybe just color enthusiasts!) to get a better understanding of how the values of Red, Blue, and Green interact on our computers.

The Rules

You have 10 seconds to get the bottom color as close to the top color as you can by inputting the values for red, green, and blue. Player with the highest score wins!
